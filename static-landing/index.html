Manifesto

The Philosophy of Coherence

"Your mind is for having ideas, not holding them. Yet your ideas scatter when your tools divide them."

01 / The Cost of Drift

Defeat attention fragmentation

Every time you jump between six disconnected applications to capture a note, record a habit, edit a project, and review flashcards, your cognitive load increases. Hulu eliminates the friction of context-switching by combining everything into a unified operational layer.

02 / The Power of Loops

Deliberate review pipelines

Captured thoughts only become valuable if they are actively integrated. Hulu enforces structured midday, evening, and weekly audit queues to convert raw captures into tasks, knowledge base files, or learning syllabi, preventing backlog accumulation.

03 / Compound Learning

Spaced repetition integration

Don't let insights slip away. Hulu pairs standard web research with an internal spaced repetition (SRS) memory engine. Note details flow straight into study courses, generating flashcards automatically to build permanent, retrievable knowledge.

The Story

Built for real personal operations, not a VC pitch.

We live in a hyper-fragmented ecosystem. We dump loose ideas in Apple Notes, coordinate tasks in Todoist, track habits in Streaks, reflect in Day One, research topics in Notion, and query ChatGPT in a separate tab.

Our attention is split across 6 disconnected tools, and our knowledge fails to compound. Hulu (ሁሉ) was born from this exact friction.

It is a single, integrated environment that treats life management like a high-integrity Operating System. It combines structured databases, spaced repetition learning matrixes, and local AI agents into a single, cohesive framework. No distractions, pure momentum.

Rhythm of operations

A Day In Hulu

07:00 / Align

Morning Intentions

Wake up to a clean, focused broadsheet. State your core focus for the day, check active schedules, and log initial wellness markers (mood, sleep hours) on the ledger.

12:00 / Ingest

Frictionless Capture

As you read, browse, or code, throw raw notes, URLs, images, or snippets into the Inbox Capture Desk. Keep your immediate focus clear; let local heuristics and AI categorize details for later.

14:00 / Execute

Focus Pomodoro Block

Fire up deep-work sessions. Link focus timers directly to execution tasks on your Kanban board. Custom AudioContext synths signal end-of-block transitions automatically.

21:00 / Reflect

Evening Review & Spaced Study

Perform an end-of-day audit. Log daily lessons in the markdown editor, review flashcards scheduled by the spaced repetition (SRS) queue, and inspect your composite daily Life Score.

Sunday / Audit

Weekly Sprint Audit

Enter the Review Room. Clear remaining captures, archive completed projects, reset habit grids, and flag database staleness to plan the upcoming sprint cycle.

Engineering Specs

High-Integrity Technology Stack

Framework

Next.js 16

Engineered with Turbopack and React 19 to guarantee instant rendering, zero layout shifts, and sub-100ms route transitions.

Database

Supabase

PostgreSQL schema relations and real-time subscription channels sync data changes immediately across views.

Intelligence

Gemini 1.5 Flash

AI pipelines crawl Exa search results, auto-categorize inbox slips, and build custom study course outlines on-demand.

Sound Synth

Web Audio API

Audio notifications synthesize tones directly in-browser using sine wave oscillators, removing heavy asset dependencies.

Your life data is yours alone. Protected by Row Level Security.

Hulu is architected with strict PostgreSQL Row Level Security (RLS) policies. Every query payload is strictly bounded by `auth.uid() = user_id`, meaning database records are isolated to your account. No tenant data leaks, no telemetry, and complete encryption.
